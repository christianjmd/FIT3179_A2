The World Happiness Report is a publication from the Sustainable Development Solutions Network that ranks countries by their happiness levels.

Scores from this report are generated from the responses of the citizens of the country. Questions are to be answered on a scale from 1 - 10 based on their experience on 6 factors - economic production, social support, life expectancy, freedom, absence of corruption, and generosity.

This visualisation aims to show the changes in happiness of each country over the past 5 years.
